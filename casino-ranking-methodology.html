Scoring weights

The ranking balances game choice, bonus clarity, payment experience, mobile usability, support quality and player protection tools. No single bonus headline should outweigh weak payments or unclear limits.

  • 30 percent games: slots, live tables, providers, search and game information.
  • 25 percent payments: deposit methods, withdrawal speed, fees and limits.
  • 20 percent bonus clarity: wagering, expiry, game weighting and maximum cashout.
  • 15 percent player tools: deposit limits, time-outs, reminders and history.
  • 10 percent support: contact options, speed and answer quality.
